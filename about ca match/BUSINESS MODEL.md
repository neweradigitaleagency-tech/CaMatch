8.2 REVENUE STREAMS
2.1 Transaction Fees (Phase 1-2)
Commission Structure:
BASE COMMISSION: 12% of GMV

DISCOUNTS BY VERIFICATION LEVEL:
├── Level 2 (ID Verified):     12%
├── Level 3 (Background):      12%
├── Level 4 (Certified):       10%
└── Level 5 (Elite):            8%

VOLUME DISCOUNTS (Monthly):
├── 0-50 jobs:                 Base rate
├── 51-100 jobs:              -1%
├── 101-200 jobs:             -2%
└── 200+ jobs:                -3%

EXAMPLE CALCULATION:
Job value: 20,000 XOF
Pro level: Certified (10%)
Monthly volume: 75 jobs (-1%)
Effective rate: 9%
Platform fee: 1,800 XOF
Pro receives: 18,200 XOF 
Payment Method Fees:
├── Wave:           0% (absorbed by platform)
├── Orange Money:   1.5% (passed to pro or absorbed)
├── MTN MoMo:       2% (passed to pro or absorbed)
├── Cash:           0% (but pro must top up wallet)
└── Credit Card:    3.5% (passed to client) 
Projected Revenue (Year 1):
Month 1-3:   $50K GMV/mo  × 12% = $6K/mo
Month 4-6:   $150K GMV/mo × 11% = $16.5K/mo
Month 7-9:   $350K GMV/mo × 10% = $35K/mo
Month 10-12: $600K GMV/mo × 10% = $60K/mo

Year 1 Total: ~$350K

2.3 Financial Products (Phase 3+)
Embedded Financing:
MICRO-LOANS
├── Amount: 50,000 - 500,000 XOF
├── Purpose: Equipment, inventory, expansion
├── Term: 3-12 months
├── Interest: 2-5% per month (declining)
├── Underwriting: Based on platform performance
└── Revenue: Interest + origination fee (2%)

EXAMPLE:
Loan: 200,000 XOF
Term: 6 months
Interest: 3%/month
Total repayment: 236,000 XOF
Platform revenue: 36,000 XOF (interest) + 4,000 XOF (fee) = 40,000 XOF 
Micro-Insurance:
JOB INSURANCE
├── Coverage: Property damage, injuries
├── Premium: 500 XOF per job (deducted from fee)
├── Payout: Up to 200,000 XOF per incident
├── Partner: Local insurer (NSIA, AXA CI)
└── Revenue: Commission (20% of premium)

EQUIPMENT INSURANCE
├── Coverage: Tools and equipment
├── Premium: 2-5% of equipment value per year
├── Payout: Replacement cost
└── Revenue: Commission (15% of premium) Projected Revenue (Year 2):
Micro-loans: $500K portfolio × 15% yield = $75K
Insurance: 50,000 jobs × 500 XOF × 20% = $5K
Total Year 2: ~$80K
