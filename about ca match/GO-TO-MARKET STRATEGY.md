9.3 MARKETING & ACQUISITION STRATEGY
3.1 Client Acquisition Channels
Digital Marketing (40% of CAC)
Social Media: Facebook & Instagram ads targeting Cocody/Plateau residents. Focus on video testimonials and "before/after" cleaning/repair content.
Influencer Marketing: Partner with local Ivorian lifestyle influencers and comedians for relatable, humorous ads about the struggles of finding a good plumber.
Search: Google Ads for high-intent keywords ("plombier urgence Abidjan", "électricien Cocody").
Content/SEO: Blog posts on home maintenance tips in French and Nouchi.
Offline & Guerrilla Marketing (30% of CAC)
Flyering & Posters: High-footfall areas (supermarkets like Prosuma, Cap Sud; malls like Playce).
Radio: Sponsorship of morning shows on top stations (Trafic Info, Nostalgie CI) with "Tip of the day" segments.
Branded Vehicles: Wrap 10 top-performing pros' vehicles/scooters with Ça Match branding. They become moving billboards.
Referral & Loyalty (20% of CAC)
Double-Sided Referral: "Invitez un ami, gagnez 2 000 XOF". Both get credit towards their next job.
Loyalty Program: Earn points for every job, redeemable for discounts or free maintenance checks.
Partnerships (10% of CAC)
Real Estate Agencies: Partner with agencies to offer Ça Match as the preferred maintenance partner for new tenants.
Hardware Stores: Cross-promotions with quincailleries (e.g., "Buy materials at X, get 10% off labor on Ça Match").
3.2 Professional Acquisition Strategy (Supply Side)
Crucial: You can't acquire clients without pros.
Field Recruitment (Ground Game)
Scouting Teams: Hire 5 "Community Managers" to visit hardware stores, construction sites, and informal worker hubs in Abobo, Yopougon, and Adjamé.
Onboarding Events: Host weekend "BBQ & Tech" events where pros can register, get their photo taken, and learn the app over food and drinks.
Incentives: "First 500 pros get 0% commission for 3 months" and a free branded t-shirt.
Digital & Word of Mouth
WhatsApp Groups: Infiltrate and provide value in existing WhatsApp groups for electricians/plumbers in Abidjan.
Pro Referrals: "Invite a colleague, get 5 000 XOF when they complete their first 5 jobs."
Verification as a Benefit
Position the "Ça Match Verified" badge as a mark of prestige. Pros will join just to get the badge to use on their own offline marketing.
3.3 Enterprise Sales Strategy (B2B)
Target: Hotels, residential complexes, corporate offices, retail chains.
Sales Process:
Lead Gen: LinkedIn outreach to Facility Managers, HR Directors, and Operations Directors.
Discovery: Understand their current pain points (managing 50+ independent contractors, tracking SLAs, invoice reconciliation).
Pilot: Offer a 30-day free pilot for one site (e.g., one hotel).
ROI Presentation: Show time saved and cost reduction.
Close: Annual contract with SLA guarantees.
9.4 PARTNERSHIP STRATEGY
4.1 Strategic Alliances
Mobile Money Providers (Wave, Orange Money, MTN)
Goal: Seamless payments, co-marketing.
Action: Negotiate zero-fee merchant status with Wave. Co-branded campaigns ("Pay with Wave, get 500 XOF cashback").
Insurance Companies (NSIA, AXA Côte d'Ivoire)
Goal: Micro-insurance for jobs.
Action: Integrate API for per-job insurance. Market "Insured Jobs" as a massive trust signal to clients.
Vocational Training Centers
Goal: Supply of certified, high-quality pros.
Action: Create a "Ça Match Certified" track. Top graduates get fast-tracked to Level 4 verification and featured placement.
Hardware & Material Suppliers
Goal: Procurement marketplace (Phase 3) and pro discounts.
Action: Negotiate B2B discounts for pros buying through the app.
9.5 LAUNCH PLAN
5.1 Phase 1: Closed Beta (Month 1-2)
Scope: 50 pros, 200 clients (Friends, family, network).
Area: Cocody only (Riviera, Angré, 2 Plateaux).
Goal: Test core flows, fix critical bugs, gather testimonials.
Marketing: Zero public marketing. Manual matching if AI fails.
5.2 Phase 2: Soft Launch (Month 3)
Scope: 500 pros, 2,000 clients.
Area: Cocody + Plateau.
Goal: Prove unit economics, test payment flows, refine matching algorithm.
Marketing: Targeted Facebook ads, micro-influencers, PR in local tech blogs (Abidjan.net).
5.3 Phase 3: Grand Launch (Month 4)
Scope: 2,000 pros, 20,000 clients.
Area: All of Abidjan.
Goal: Market dominance, liquidity in all 4 categories.
Marketing:
Launch Event: Invite press, influencers, government officials (MINPOSTEL).
Billboards: 10 strategic billboards across Abidjan (Autoroute du Nord, VGE, Pont HKB).
Radio Blitz: 2-week intensive radio campaign.
Promo: "First job cleaning at 50% off" (subsidized by marketing budget).
5.4 Phase 4: Expansion Prep (Month 9-12)
Goal: Prepare for Dakar and Accra.
Actions: Localize app (currency, language nuances), establish local legal entities, recruit country managers.
9.6 RETENTION & GROWTH STRATEGY
6.1 Client Retention
Push Notifications: "It's been 3 months since your AC was cleaned. Book now to save energy!" (Predictive maintenance).
Subscription Model (Phase 2): "Ça Match Pass" - 5 000 XOF/month for 1 free cleaning + 20% off all repairs.
Customer Support: WhatsApp-first support. Resolve issues in < 2 hours.
6.2 Professional Retention
Financial Inclusion: Offer micro-loans for tools once they hit 50 jobs.
Gamification: Badges, leaderboards, "Pro of the Month" with cash bonuses.
Community: Host quarterly meetups with food, drinks, and training sessions. Make them feel part of a union/guild.
SaaS Stickiness: Once they use Ça Match for invoicing and scheduling, switching costs become very high.
6.3 Enterprise Retention
Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs): Show SLA performance, cost savings, and sustainability metrics.
Dedicated Slack/WhatsApp Channel: Instant access to their account manager.
Custom Reporting: Automated monthly PDF reports for their finance departments.
9.7 GTM METRICS & KPIs
7.1 Acquisition Metrics
CAC (Client & Pro): Target < $5 for clients, < $20 for pros.
Activation Rate: % of signups who complete first job (Target: 40% clients, 60% pros).
Viral Coefficient (K-factor): Target > 1.2 for referrals.
7.2 Engagement Metrics
MAU/DAU Ratio: Target > 30% for pros (they should open app daily).
Match Rate: % of requests that get at least 3 quotes (Target: 85%).
Time to Match: Average time from request to pro acceptance (Target: < 15 mins).
7.3 Financial Metrics
GMV Growth: MoM growth rate (Target: 20%+).
Take Rate: Blended commission + SaaS (Target: 12% → 18% over 12 months).
LTV:CAC Ratio: Target > 5:1.
7.4 Quality Metrics
NPS (Net Promoter Score): Target > 50 for both clients and pros.
CSAT (Customer Satisfaction): Target > 4.5/5 stars average.
Dispute Rate: Target < 2% of total jobs.
9.8 RISK MITIGATION IN GTM
Risk
Impact
Mitigation Strategy
Supply/Demand Imbalance
High
If too many clients, expand pro recruitment. If too many pros, subsidize client demand (discounts).
Disintermediation (Leakage)
High
Pros and clients exchanging numbers to avoid fees. Mitigate by keeping escrow, offering warranty only on-platform, and penalizing off-platform behavior.
Quality Control at Scale
High
Strict verification, AI quality scoring, and rapid banning of bad actors. "Three strikes" policy.
Telco/API Downtime
Medium
Fallback to cash payments, manual reconciliation, multi-provider strategy.
Competitor Copycat
Medium
Move fast. Lock in enterprise contracts with long-term SLAs. Build deep SaaS features that take months to copy.
